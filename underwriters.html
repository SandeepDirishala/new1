Our Underwriters

Although we partner with a variety of organizations, the majority of student insurance products are now underwritten by the UnitedHealthcare Insurance Company.

UnitedHealthcare Insurance Company

The UnitedHealthcare Insurance Company is the underwriter of insurance provided by UnitedHealthcare Services, a subsidiary of Minnetonka, Minnesota-based healthcare giant UnitedHealth Group. UnitedHealthcare Insurance Company underwrites insurance plans which cover students, individuals and small to midsized employer groups across the country (except in New York State, where insurance is underwritten by UnitedHealthcare Insurance Company of New York). UnitedHealth Group‘s Health Care Services segment manages HMO, PPO, and POS (point-of-service) plans, as well as various Medicare and Medicaid options for more than 18 million medical members.
