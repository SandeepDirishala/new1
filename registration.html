2015-1 Summary Documents

HealthiestYou: Telehealth

Doctors are making house calls again! Included with your student insurance plan, and provided by HealthiestYou, is 24/7/365 access to board-certified physicians online or on the phone (where permitted). Totally worth it for minor stuff (like allergies, pink eye and earaches), the doctors on Telehealth can even send your prescription to your preferred pharmacy. The future is here!
Telehealth can be a win/win:

  • Talk to a licensed physician 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  • Pay no consultation fee.
  • Save money and time.
  • Toll free # is on your SR ID card or in My Account

Discount Plan

This idea is pretty simple: Save between 5% and 50% on healthcare-related items that may not be covered under your student insurance plan. From gym memberships to teeth whitening – the UnitedHealth Allies discount program saves you money at the point of purchase. It’s not insurance. There are no claim forms and there’s no limit to how much you can save every year!
Save on:

  • Cosmetic dental services (ready for that WHITE smile?)
  • Gym memberships, active apparel and fitness equipment
  • Annual eye exams, eyeglasses, contact lenses, even LASIK vision correction surgery
  • Natural foods, teas, vitamins and supplements, and skin care products
  • Learn more at www.sr.unitedhealthallies.com.

Global Emergency Services

One of our superheroes, GES tells great stories about saving the day for students who are studying or traveling in other states or other countries. When you’re at least 100 miles from home and you’re sick or hurt, GES steps in and steps up with immediate access to doctors, hospitals and pharmacies in unfamiliar places. In the extreme, they’ll even help you get you home if you need to be medically evacuated.
GES’ superpowers include:

  • Medical Assistance Service
  • Medical Evacuation and Repatriation Services
  • Security and Natural Disaster
  • Evacuation Services
  • Worldwide Destination Intelligence
  • Travel Assistance Services
  • Toll free # is on your SR ID card or in My Account

Student Assistant Program

College just isn’t easy! SAP counselors are there to help you deal with everything from missing your mom to helping review your lease. Call any time, any day. Talk to us about anything and everything – it’s all totally confidential! Not so comfortable on the phone? No worries – SAP’s online too.
Included with your student insurance plan, call if you need:

  • Someone to talk to
  • A lawyer’s advice
  • Answers with financial questions
  • To work something out in mediation
  • Toll free # is on your SR ID card or in My Account

Nurseline

Being in charge of your own health is actually a big deal, you’ll probably have a lot of questions. Call the team of registered nurses at NurseLine any time to get answers. Knowledge equals power! Call Nurseline and take control of your healthcare.
Call NurseLine when you:

  • Aren’t sure if you have a serious medical problem
  • Want to learn how to take care of a new or chronic condition
  • Need information about medications, tests or procedures
  • Want to find new ways to talk effectively with your health care provider
  • Toll free # is on your SR ID card or in My Account
