It’s the law!


This may be one of the least interesting sections of the site, but these rules and regulations apply to all insurance companies. They help protect consumers and make sure that insurance companies are managed properly so that they can keep their promise to help you pay for covered benefits in return for your premium.

Pick a section at left to learn more about our Privacy Policies or state specific regulations. If you would like a hard copy of our Privacy Policies, or if you have any questions about health insurance regulations in your state, call the Customer Service number on your SR ID card and we’ll be happy to assist.
