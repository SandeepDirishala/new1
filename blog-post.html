Important Tax Return Documents

News / NOV 5 2015

Within the coming weeks, UHCSR members will recieve communication that their 2015 Form 1095-b is ready for policies that meets the definitions under the Affordable care act (ACA). this is the IRS form that is needed to show minimum essential coverage for 2015.

International students on F,J,Q and M visas have a "nonresident alien" tax status and likely did not have to purchase and insurance plan that is compliant with ACA and will not recieve a 1095-B form
